Lead Taker Tool
Your step-by-step quick-start guide
This one tool takes a lead from the first phone call all the way to a signed agreement — it reads your notes, checks availability, creates the HubSpot deal, thanks the referral partner, and generates the Client Service Agreement. Here’s the whole flow in seven steps.
First time · one-time setup
Sign in to all three services in the top-right — Microsoft (to email from your Outlook), HubSpot (to load your leads & create deals as you), and DocuSign (to send agreements as you). You only do this once.
The 7-step workflow
1
Paste your call notes
There are no forms to fill out. Drop everything the caller told you into the box at the top — who called, who needs care, where, the schedule, when to start, and the referral source — then click Parse with AI. It reads the notes and fills the availability check and the entire deal form below.
2
Check availability
The coverage, schedule, and address come pre-filled from your notes. Confirm them, set Driver needed? to “Must drive” if the client needs a caregiver who drives, then click Check Availability for an instant staffing answer.
3
Tell the caller
You get a clear verdict: YES means you can confirm on the spot; Hedge means a few days need a quick look. The Say to the caller box gives you warm, ready-to-read wording in your own voice — no “let me check with scheduling.”
4
Review the auto-filled deal
Scroll to Lead details — the AI has already filled the caller, client, schedule, and summary from your notes. Just review and tweak anything that needs it.
5
Link the referral source
In Source & referral, search the referral partner’s name to associate them with the deal and grab their email. You’re auto-selected as the lead taker. Then click Create deal & post to Teams.
6
Thank the referral source
Right below the deal, the Thank the referral source box drafts a warm, partnership thank-you in Tribute’s voice — pre-addressed to the partner you linked. Review it and Send from your own Outlook.
7
Generate the Client Service Agreement
When a lead reaches Qualified, open the Lead Desk · CSAs tab and pick it. The schedule and rates are auto-filled and the right CSA template is pre-selected. Double-check the client’s email, then Generate the DocuSign draft — it sends as you and advances the deal to Contract Sent.
A few tips. The orange arrows above point to the one thing that matters in each step. Everything the AI fills is editable — always give it a quick review before you send. And remember the house language: we say “visit,” never “shift.”
