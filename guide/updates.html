T Availability & Lead Tool — What's New
A round of upgrades based on your feedback. Here's what changed.
Thanks for the early feedback — it shaped all of this. A few quick highlights, then the full list:
📞 A simpler answer on the call
The result is now built for live calls: can you confirm, the days to confirm now, the days to hedge with scheduling, and the exact words to say. The detailed grid and caregiver specifics are tucked behind "▾ Show availability details" — out of the way during the call.
✉️ New: automatic referral thank-you
When you create a deal that has a referral source, the tool drafts a personalized thank-you (specific to that client and referral — not a template) and auto-emails it from your Outlook to the right RDM for that market, ready for them to review and send to the partner. Subject: "New referral from …"
📝 The deal form fills itself — more completely
  • Every lead field now auto-fills from your notes — caller, client, referral, lead source, market, salesperson, and more.
  • General Summary and Details about client are now distinct (no more duplicate text).
  • Added Start Date of Service; pick a Deal stage (Closed-Won / Lost / Turndown) and the matching reason fields appear.
  • Next steps only fill when the caller actually states one — no more invented info.
⚡ Three ways to submit + your own Teams
Choose Create HubSpot deal, Post to Teams, or both at once. Sign in with Microsoft once and the Teams post goes to your own team & channel, as you — and the deal is created owned by you.
🎨 Fresh look
Cleaner, lighter interface that matches the rest of our tools.
