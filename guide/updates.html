T Availability & Lead Tool — What's New
A fresh round of upgrades from your feedback. Here's what changed.
It still starts from one notes box — paste everything the caller told you, hit ✨ Parse with AI, and it fills the deal form for you.
📝 The deal form fills itself — more completely
Every lead field auto-fills from your notes — caller, client, referral, lead source, market, salesperson. General Summary and Details about client are now distinct (no duplicate text), there's a Start Date of Service field, and picking a Deal stage (Closed-Won / Lost / Turndown) reveals the matching reason fields. Review, tweak, done.
⚡ Three ways to submit + your own Teams
Create HubSpot deal, Post to Teams, or both at once. Sign in with Microsoft once and the Teams post goes to your own team & channel, as you — and the deal is created owned by you.
🤝 RDMs automatically looped in on referrals
Since RDMs aren't in the tool, the moment you create a deal with a referral source, the right RDM for that market is automatically emailed a ready-to-send thank-you — including the partner's email address and a suggested subject — so they can send it on with one quick review. Nothing falls through the cracks.
🗂️ New: Lead Desk — send the CSA in a couple clicks
A new Lead Desk shows your team's Qualified leads waiting on a Client Service Agreement. Pick one and the tool builds the CSA from the right regional template, pre-filled with the client's details, then opens it in DocuSign to review and send — as you. Once it's sent, the deal automatically advances to Contract Sent. (One-time: Connect DocuSign.)
